About Me

My name is Taddes Korris and I'm a full-stack web developer. I've worked on a variety of applications that give me a good understanding of front-end and back-end technologies. I am interested in front-end and back-end development opportunities and have experience with several programming languages and their libraries. My front-end expertise includes: JavaScript, Vue.js, React.js, jQuery, HTML5, CSS3, SASS, Bootstrap, and Materialize. My back-end knowledge includes: Node.js, Node Express, Mongoose, Sequelize, as well as relational and non-relational databases, including MongoDB, MySQL and Firebase/Google Firestore.

I am known for my persistent drive to learn new things, which includes languages, libraries and their intricacies. I have taken the initiative to learn new materials without prompt and do so with great excitement. This is why I am excited about opportunities to work together with other developers in an innovative environment.

I have a background as a professional orchestral musician, which has given me numerous skills that contribute to my abilities as a programmer. I am known for my ability to juggle many projects, learn new materials at great speed, and am rapidly adaptable to changing circumstances. Most of all, I am detail-oriented and focused on how to solve complex problems, which is a cornerstone of being a successful classical musician or programmer.
